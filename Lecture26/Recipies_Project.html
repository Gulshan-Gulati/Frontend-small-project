Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, ghee, and soda to form dough.
  2. Shape into balls and fry until golden brown.
  3. Boil sugar and water for syrup, add flavorings.
  4. Soak fried balls in syrup for a few hours.

ICE Cream

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Condensed milk
  • Vanilla extract

Recipe

  1. Whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  2. Fold in sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract.
  3. Customize with chocolate chips, crushed cookies, or fruit puree.
  4. Freeze until firm, then serve and enjoy your homemade ice cream!
